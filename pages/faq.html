Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Does It Take To Complete SEWA's Welding Program?

The programs at SEWA last approximately 2 1/2 months (360 hours) with students attending classes Monday through Friday from 8:00am to 4:00pm.

What Types Of Welding Courses Does SEWA Offer?

We offer Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW), Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW), known informally as Stick Welding, and Oxy-Fuel Welding and Cutting.

Does SEWA Offer Financial Assistance?

Yes, SEWA has in house financing with easy payment plans. Also, there are other financing options that are available for those who qualify.

What Is The Cost Of The Program?

At SEWA we offer low cost affordable programs. With our top notch instructors teaching the highest quality welding skills at industry standards we have made our programs affordable.
